How It Works

Three steps to a clear decision

No spreadsheets. No endless pros-and-cons lists. Just a structured process that works.

🎯

Define your decision

Name your choice, add criteria that matter, and list your options. AI can suggest both for you.

⚖️

Compare what matters

Rate pairs of criteria and options side by side. The same method NASA and Boeing rely on for mission-critical calls.

📊

Get ranked results

See your options ranked with confidence scores, AI executive summary, and a Devil's Advocate challenge.
